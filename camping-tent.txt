1) The zipper should be doubled so you can open it from bottom or from top.
2) It should be light, however note that ultra lightweight camping tents are
made for professional campers, so their materials are easy to damage if you
chooses an inappropriate camping spot or just do stupid things.
3) If the tent is for >=3 people make sure to choose one with 2 entrances,
so you dont have to jump over each other.
4) Good names:
- Big Agnes
- MSR (Mountain Safety Research)
5) A great overview (russian): www.kovinov.com/snarjazhenie/moe/palatki/
